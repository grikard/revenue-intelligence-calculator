# Revenue Intelligence Economic Multiplier

An interactive ROIC calculator for Revenue Intelligence and enterprise AI investment scenarios.

## Purpose

This calculator shows how small improvements in revenue performance can create outsized returns when applied to a large revenue base.

The model translates four inputs into financial outputs:

- Revenue base
- Improvement lever
- Gross margin
- AI investment

It then calculates:

- Incremental revenue
- Incremental gross profit
- ROIC multiple
- Payback period
- Year 3 compound effect

## Core Formula

```text
ROIC = (Revenue Base × Improvement Lever × Gross Margin) ÷ AI Investment
